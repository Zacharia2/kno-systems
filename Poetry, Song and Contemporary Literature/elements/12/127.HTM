Title: 议论文模板（全）

备注：这个模板给出的思路是最稳妥，最中规中矩的思路。大家都可以参考。对于英语比较好的同学，完全可以自己发挥。对于英语不太好，自己实在写不出来的同学可以背这几个模板，然后根据具体题目适当补充内容。模板背完之后一定要多写几篇作文练习一下到底怎么用。这个是关键。

1．  有提纲

按照提纲分段表述，有几条提纲，写几段，且每一段 第一句话是对提纲的翻译。

如2021年真题，How to bridge the digital gap for the elderly based on the following information.

The digital has reduced the social participation of the elderly in recent years. Many of them have difficulties in using smart devices.

Your essay should include:

1 Your understanding of this phenomenon.

2.Measures that should be taken to help the elderly.

 

2.没有提纲，只有主题，如：on mobile payment, on clean your plate campaign. On excessive package.  how to avoid internet addiction. How to reach carbon neutrality. Why should we reach carbon neutrality.

     分类审题：

A． 对于有争议性的话题：如on we-media   (作业：on square dance)

第一段：描述这种现象。With the development of the society, …名词短语becomes more and more common. It has attracted attention.

第二段：正反面分析：好处 123    坏处123

Different people have different opinions. Some people think it is good. There are some reasons. First,…句子…  Second,句子…..Third,句子… Some people think it is bad. There are some reasons. First,…句子Second,句子…Third,…句子

第三段，你怎么认为。As far as I am concerned, I think…句子  Every coin has two sides. We should make use of its advantages and at the same time, we should avoid the bad effects. （共77单词）

 

Example:

On We Media

With the development of society, we media becomes more and more common. It has attracted attention.

Different people have different ideas. Some people like it. Some others don’t like it.

Some people think it is good. There are several reasons. First, it is a good way for us to relax. There are many interesting videos on we media. When you are tired, you can watch these videos. They can make you relaxed. Second, we can share our life with others. Some others think it is bad. There are several reasons. First, it costs too much time. Some young people waste too much time on we media. Second, it influences our study.

As far as I am concerned, I think it has advantages and disadvantages. Every coin has two sides. We should make good use of it and at the same time, we should avoid the bad effects.

 

 

 

B.对于好的现象。如on clean your plate campaign   (作业 On a harmonious Dormitory life)

第一段，描述这种现象。With the development of the society, 名词短语…becomes more and more common. It has attracted attention.

第二段，论述它能给我们带来什么好处。It can bring many benefits to our life. First,句子….Second, 句子.Third,句子

第三段，如何做才能实现。There are many measures we can take to realize it. First, … second,… third,…

第四段，结论。All in all, I think 名词短语…is good. On one hand, it can improve our life. On the other hand, it can improve the development of our society. We should take more measures to encourage people to do it. Only by doing this can we make our society a better one. (共76单词)

 

 

Example:

On Clean Your Plate Campaign

With the development of the society, “Clean Your Plate Campaign”becomes more and more common. It has attracted attention.

It can bring many benefits to our life. First, it can help us save food. If you want to clean your plate, you have to eat all the food on the plate. So, nothing is wasted. Second, it can help people form a good habit. Thrift is good to us.

There are many measures we can take to realize it. First, parents and schools should teach children to clean their plate. Children should form this good habit. Second, as college students, we should clean our plate at every meal. Third, we should advise our friends to clean their plates, too.

All in all, I think “Clean Your Plate Campaign” is good. On one hand, it can improve our life. On the other hand, it can improve the development of our society. We should take more measures to encourage people to do it. Only by doing this can we make our society a better one. (共140单词)

 

 

C.对于不好的现象。如On excessive package.

第一段，描述这种现象。With the development of the society, …名词短语becomes more and more common. It has attracted attention.

第二段，论述它能给我们带来什么坏处。It can bring many harms to our life. First, 句子….Second,句子….Third, 句子

第三段，如何做才能避免这种现象。There are many measures we can take to avoid it. First, … second,… third,…

 

第四段，结论。All in all, I think 名词短语…is bad. Everyone should avoid its effect. Also, our government should take more measures to remind the people not to do it any more. Only by doing this can we make our society a better one.（共77单词）

 

Example:

             On Network Loan 

With the development of the society, network loan becomes more and more common. It has attracted attention.

It can bring many harms to our life. First, the interest is very high. It is much higher than the interest in the banks. Second, it will give us much pressure. If you use network loan, you have to pay much money back. It is a heavy burden to us. Third, you can’t protect your private information if you use network loan.

There are many measures we can take to avoid it. First, we should spend money according to our economic situations. For example, if your family is rich, you can spend more money. If your family is not rich, you should not spend too much money. Second, we should live a simple life. we should resist the temptation of luxurious life.

All in all, I think network loan is bad. Everyone should avoid its effect. Also, our government should take more measures to remind the people not to do it any more. Only by doing this can we make our society a better one.（共122单词）

 

 

 

 

 

D．how to 。。如：How to avoid internet addiction. How to reach carbon neutrality. （作业 How to keep psychologically healthy）

 

第一段，描述这种现象。With the development of the society, how to+动词短语….has attracted attention. People give different solutions to this problem.

第二段，给出解决方案。In my opinion, I think we should take different measures to solve this problem. First,….Second, the government should make laws to help solve this problem. Third,

第三段，总结。All in all, there are many ways to solve this problem. If we want to solve it quickly, we should make a joint effort. I am sure if everybody can do his best, this problem will be solved soon. （共79个单词）

 

Example:

How to Avoid Internet Addiction

With the development of the society, how to avoid internet addiction has attracted attention. People give different solutions to this problem.

In my opinion, I think we should take different measures to solve this problem. First, we should not use our mobile phone all the time. We should put down the phone and do some exercises. We should do some outdoor activities. Second, the government should make laws to help solve this problem. For example, the laws has forced computer games companies to limit the computer game time of the young children. Third, the society should make more people know the harm of internet addiction. For example, we can put on posters in schools to tell students it is not good to be addicted to internet.

All in all, there are many ways to solve this problem. If we want to solve it quickly, we should make a joint effort. I am sure if everybody can do his best, this problem will be solved soon. （共89个单词）

 

 

E：why …类作文Why should we reach carbon neutrality. (作业  why more and more people do volunteering activities?)

第一段，            描述这种现象。With the development of the society, 某现象 has attracted attention. It has appeared for a long time. But now it is more and more important.

第二段，给出原因。In my opinion, I think there are several reasons. First,…..Second,…. Third,….

第三段 总结。All in all, 某现象is very important to our development. Only when it is properly handled can we have a better future. （共57个单词）

 

 

Example:

Why Should We Reach Carbon Neutrality

With the development of the society, carbon neutrality has attracted attention. It has appeared for a long time. But now it is more and more important.

In my opinion, I think there are several reasons. First, it is very important to our environment. Now, the green house effect has become more and more serious. Carbon neutrality is the most effective way to reduce green house effect. Second, it can help us save many plants and animals. The global temperature is higher and higher. Many plants and animals are threatened by the change of climate. By carbon neutrality, we can stop the trend of climate change and get a stable environment. 

All in all, to reach carbon neutrality is very important to our development. Only when it is properly handled can we have a better future.
