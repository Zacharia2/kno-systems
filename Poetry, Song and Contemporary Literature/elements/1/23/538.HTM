英语范文---3 月 24 日 Recently we made a survey of the people on physical training．Only 35 percent of the surveyed people have had sports but the rest have never． Do you know why?There are several reasons for this． More than 10％of them don’t know how to train．More than 10％of them think the places where they can do exercise are so far away from their houses that it is not convenient for them to do exercise．Over 30％of them complain that there is not enough space or facilities．Even more than 10％ of them say that they haven’t got enough time． Lack of physical training will not keep people healthy．It is necessary for people to realize the importance of physical training．Some measures should be taken to provide people with training facilities and places． In a word, everyone should take part in physical training in order to keep himself healthy．

译：最近我们做了一个关于体育锻炼的人的调查。只有 35%的受访者参加过体育运动，其余的人则从未参加过。你知道为什么吗?这有几个原因。其中超过 10%的人不知道如何训练。超过 10%的人认为他们可以锻炼的地方离他们的房子太远，这对他们锻炼不方便。超过 30%的人抱怨没有足够的空间或设施。甚至超过 10%的人说他们没有足够的时间。缺乏体育锻炼不能使人保持健康。人们有必要认识到体育锻炼的重要性。应该采取一些措施为人们提供培训设施和场所。总而言之，为了保持健康，每个人都应该参加体育锻炼
