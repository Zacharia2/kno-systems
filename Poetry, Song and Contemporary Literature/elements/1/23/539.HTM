英语范文（关于冬奥会）---3 月 19 日 Beijing Olympic Games

Beijing Olympic Games has brought so many surprises to the world, we show our new image to the world. Many Chinese heroes fought for the right to host the Olympic Games and we succeeded in the end. Now we have successfully won the bid to host the Winter Olympics. Our persistence has helped us create so many miracles. We are so proud that Beijing has become the only city so far to host both summer and Winter Olympic Games, but the road to realize this amazing phenomenon is so difficult. And because we persevered, we won the vote and made that dream come true. Two days ago, we won the vote to host the Winter Olympics. Our country has always worked miracles. Persistent effort brings good luck. I believe we can work more miracles.

译：北京奥运会给世界带来了如此多的惊喜，我们向世界展示了我们的新形象。许多中国英雄为举办奥运会的权利而斗争，最终我们成功了。现在我们也成功地赢得了冬季奥运会的举办权。我们的坚持帮助我们创造了如此多的奇迹。北京已经成为迄今为止唯一举办夏季和冬季奥运会的城市，我们很自豪，但实现这一惊人现象的道路是如此艰难。由于坚持不懈的努力，我们赢得了选票，实现了这个梦想。两天前，我们又赢得了举办冬季奥运会的投票。我们国家一直在创造奇迹，坚持不懈的努力会带来好运，我相信我们可以创造更多奇迹
