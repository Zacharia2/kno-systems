Title: 漫画作文：

1） 第一段主要是描述图画中的信息和表达的含义。

1.   描述图片：人物/动物/事物＋动作＋环境---时态 ---一般过去或一般现在

The picture shows that ….或者

From the picture we can see that a bottle is lying on the ground. There is a little wine in the bottle. One man is sad and says: “Oh dear, what a pity. Nothing is left.” The other man is happy and says : “Good. There is still some wine left.”//From the picture we can see that a teacher is teaching multiplication table. A student asks : “no offense, but......”
2. 象征寓意：

The picture shows us a very common social issue. Some people are pessimistic  while  others are  optimistic.  They have different views                     for the same problem.//The picture shows us a very common social issue. Some people think knowledge will be outdated. We should not spend much time studying knowledge. I think this view is wrong.

 

2） 第二段的全文的重点，一般都是要求具体分析图画中包含的社会现象。

    Which one do you want to be? I want to be an optimistic person. I think optimism is good for our life. There are several reasons. First, optimism can make us happy. When we are happy, we will love life more. Second, optimism can encourage us to continue with our work and study. When we meet difficulties, optimism can cheer us up and encourage us to try to find solutions.

3） 结束：可以给出自己的评论、可以提出自己的建议以及针对现象应该采取的措施。

How to be an optimistic person? I have several suggestions. First, we can communicate with our friends and families. Their love can make us an optimistic person. Second, we can read more books. Books can broaden our horizon and help us put down many burdens. Third, when we are not happy, we can do some sports and have a walk in the garden.

 

例文  红色部分可以直接用

From this picture, we can see a lot of things. There are two men in this picture. There is a bottle of water on the floor and the water spills out. One of them is very sad and pessimistic. He says everything is finished. But the other man is optimistic and he says there is still some left. We should see the truth behind the picture. In fact, this picture tells us more than what is printed here. It shows two different kinds of thinking patterns: the optimistic and the pessimistic. (描述图片描述的时候可以全篇采用一般现在时，最后一句点题--这幅图背后反映的实质是什么)

      I think optimistic is better than pessimistic. An optimistic person can always see bight side of our world. But the pessimistic person can only see the dark side of the world. It is not good for our mental health. I think everyone should try to be an optimistic person.（分析）

It is just a picture but it shows us something very important. We can learn a lot from this picture. At the same time, we should pay enough attention to it. Only in this way can we enjoy a happier life and our society will be better and better.(49个单词，占了总字数的三分之一) （总结，放之四海而皆准的正确的废话）

 

 

Ø  Tips：第二段和第三段的开头最好写一个主题句，既可以防止离题，又符合英文写作的习惯。
