Not for the first time,an argument had broken out over breakfast at number four, PrivetDrive. Mr. Vernon Dursley had been woken in the early hours of themorning by a loud, hooting noise from his nephew Harry’sroom.

“Third time this week!” he roared across the table.“If you can’t control that owl, it’ll have togo!”
