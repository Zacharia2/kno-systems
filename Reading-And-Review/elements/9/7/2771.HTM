Harry felt as though his insides had turned to ice. He lookedbehind him. Sitting on one of the desks by the wall was none otherthan Albus Dumbledore. Harry must have walked straight past him, sodesperate to get to the mirror he hadn’t noticed him.

“I — I didn’t see you, sir.”
