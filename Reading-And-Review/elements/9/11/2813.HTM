Ron had fallen asleep in the dark common room,waiting for them to return. He shouted something about Quidditchfouls when Harry roughly shook him awake. In a matter of seconds,though, he was wide-eyed as Harry began to tell him and Hermionewhat had happened in the forest.

Harry couldn’t sit down. He paced up and down in front ofthe fire. He was still shaking.

“Snape wants the Stone for Voldemort . . .and Voldemort’s waiting in the forest . . .and all this time we thought Snape just wanted to getrich. . . .”
