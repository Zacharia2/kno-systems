1、外显注意力目标开启了内隐空间统计学习

人们可以快速学习目标的空间分布，并将注意力引导到目标的可能区域。这些内隐学习的空间偏差已被证明是持久的，并能够转移到其他类似的视觉搜索任务中。然而，持续的注意力偏见与我们典型的日常环境中频繁变化的目标不相容。我们提出了一种灵活的目标特定概率提示机制来解决这种差异。我们研究了被试是否可以在五个实验中学习和利用特定目标的空间优先级图（N= 24）。在实验1中，被试更快地在目标特定的高概率位置找到目标，这符合特定目标的概率提示效应。这表明，根据当前目标，可以灵活地激活从统计学习中衍生出来的单独空间优先级。在实验2中，我们确保结果不仅仅由试验间启动驱动。在实验3中，我们确保结果是由早期注意力引导效应驱动的。在实验4中，我们将发现扩展到包括四个位置的复杂空间分布，支持在激活的空间优先级图中目标可能性的辅助表示。最后，在实验5中，我们证实了这种效应是由注意力模板的激活驱动的，而不是目标线索和空间位置之间的联想学习驱动的。我们的研究结果证明了统计学习中一种以前未被认识到的灵活性机制。特定目标的概率提示效应依赖于基于特征和基于位置的注意力的协调，利用自上而下的控制和选择历史之间传统界限的信息。

People can quickly learn spatial distributions of targets and direct attention to likely regions of targets. These implicitly learned spatial biases have been shown to be persistent, transferring to other similar visual search tasks. However, a persistent attentional bias is incompatible with frequently changing goals in our typical daily environment. We propose a flexible goal-specific probability cueing mechanism to address this discrepancy. We examined whether participants could learn and utilize target-specific spatial priority maps across five experiments (each N = 24). In Experiment 1, participants were faster to find the target at the target-specific high-probability location, in line with a goal-specific probability cueing effect. This demonstrated that separate spatial priorities derived from statistical learning can be flexibly activated based on the current goal. In Experiment 2, we ensured the results were not driven solely by intertrial priming. In Experiment 3, we ensured the results were driven by early attentional guidance effects. In Experiment 4, we extended our findings to a complex spatial distribution including four locations, supporting a sophisticated representation of target likelihood in the activated spatial priority maps. Finally, in Experiment 5, we confirmed that the effect was driven by the activation of an attentional template and not associative learning between the target cue and a spatial location. Our findings demonstrate a previously unrecognized mechanism for flexibility within statistical learning. The goal-specific probability cueing effect relies on coordination of feature-based and location-based attention, utilizing information that crosses traditional boundaries between top-down control and selection history. 

论文原文：Zhang, Z., & Carlisle, N. B. (2023). Explicit attentional goals unlock implicit spatial statistical learning. Journal of Experimental Psychology: General, 152(8), 2125–2137.
